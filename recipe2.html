Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These chocolate chip cookies are the perfect balance of chewy and crispy. They are a classic treat that can be enjoyed with a glass of milk or as a dessert after any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add to the batter with salt.
  5. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of the dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes or until edges are nicely browned.
