Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a traditional Italian dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It is creamy, comforting, and a favorite meal for many pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add the Pecorino and Parmesan cheese. Season with black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix it with the pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly mix the pasta with the egg mixture.
  6. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese on top.
